## Phase 5: QR Ticket Generation & Blob Storage

**Acceptance Criteria:** When an order is confirmed, a PDF ticket with a QR code is generated and uploaded to Blob Storage. The attendee can retrieve a download URL for their ticket.

**Azure Resources to Add:**

- Blob Storage Container: `tickets` _(within the existing Storage Account)_

**What you build:**

- Activity function: generate QR + PDF, upload to Blob Storage
- SAS URL generation so attendees download directly from Blob
- `GET /orders/{orderId}/ticket` — returns the SAS URL
