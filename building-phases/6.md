## Phase 6: Waitlist

**Acceptance Criteria:** When an event is sold out, an attendee can join the waitlist. When a seat is released, the first person on the waitlist is notified and has X minutes (configurable per event) to claim it before it moves to the next person.

**Azure Resources to Add:**

- Cosmos DB `waitlist` container

**What you build:**

- `POST /events/{eventId}/waitlist` — join waitlist
- Waitlist Orchestrator: notify via Service Bus → wait for external event (X min timer) → claim or move to next
- `POST /orders/claim/{instanceId}` — attendee claims the offered seat, raises external event on orchestrator (kick off the Purchase Orchestrator)
