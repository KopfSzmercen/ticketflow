## Phase 4: Service Bus Readiness (Emulator + Azure)

**Acceptance Criteria:** The solution is ready for Service Bus integration in both local and Azure-hosted modes. This phase is strictly prep-only: no orchestrator publish wiring and no worker-trigger business flow implementation yet.

**Azure Resources to Add:**

- Service Bus Namespace
- Service Bus Topic: `order-events`
- Subscriptions: `email-worker`, `analytics-worker`

**What you build now (prep-only):**

- Service Bus emulator stack in local `docker-compose` (emulator + SQL Edge dependency)
- App configuration and DI seams for `ServiceBusAuthMode`:
  - `Emulator`
  - `AzureCli`
  - `ManagedIdentity`
  - `DefaultAzureCredential`
- IaC module + app settings + RBAC wiring for namespace/topic/subscriptions
- Integration test harness seams to validate:
  - publish contract invocation
  - consumer invocability
  - without asserting full broker roundtrip delivery

**Deferred to later phases:**

- Publishing from durable orchestrator/activity business flow
- Production consumer trigger implementations (`email-worker`, `analytics-worker`)
